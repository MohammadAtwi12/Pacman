PAC-MAN

PAC-MAN’s yellow color and round shape are what make him so iconic!
Always cheerful and happy,
he loves to chomp anything and everything!

Ghosts

Blinky

BLINKY is the leader of the Ghosts! He is very quick and persistent.

Inky

INKY is capricious, sometimes chasing and sometimes running away
from PAC-MAN. He’s a bit shy!

Clyde

CLYDE is a free-spirited wanderer who likes to explore every corner
of the maze.

Pinky

PINKY is the strategist of the group, anticipating and ambushing
PAC-MAN at every chance!

PAC-DOTS

Eat POWER-PELLETS and the Ghosts turn to
blue! With the tables turned, PAC-MAN is free
to chase and chomp the ghosts!
